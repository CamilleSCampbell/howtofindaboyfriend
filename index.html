01 — Logline
< Notes Done
2:47 AM · Can't sleep · November 2025
series idea (hear me out)
ok so what if there's this girl. senior year. 5.0 GPA. zero boyfriends. ever. and she decides to turn her chronic singleness into a viral TikTok thesis experiment. like she's going to SCIENTIFICALLY crack the code to romance.

but then it spirals. the stunts get worse. the views go up. her friendships go down. she tanks her dream college app. and she has to choose between the audience that loves watching her fail and the people she's actually failing.

this is either the best idea i've ever had or the most unhinged. posting it before i overthink it.

03 — Why This Show

Here's why this show should exist.

📱
Scroll Culture Native
Live viewer counts tick up during the worst moments. TikTok comments scroll on screen. The algorithm rewards her desperation with followers. This isn't a show that references internet culture. It's built inside it.
🎯
A Voice, Not a Note
A female-led teen comedy about performative vulnerability and parasocial relationships written by a 21-year-old who lives in this world. Not a studio executive's idea of Gen Z. A document from inside it.
🔥
Pre-Built Virality
Every set piece is a ready-made clip, reaction video, and TikTok stitch. The marketing is baked into the narrative. This show doesn't need a campaign because it IS one.
